Design Tips for begginers and for when you need inspiration!

Let's be honest, not everyone is always full of tips and tricks and inspiration

at least not all the time.

Honestly, I don't feel super inspired all the time.

But when I do I write it down, or go and get inspired by other people's tips.

So, I hope my tips help you

     
  • Never wear pattern with patterns for eg. florals with polka dots, because too many patterns mess with your mind and irritate you alot.
    •  
  • Whenever you wear patterns always either wear a patterned shirt with plain bottoms or vice versa, if you want to wear patterns head to toe than wear the same pattern or a dress or a romper.s
    •  
  • Avoid wearing patterns on areas you don't want to highlight.
  • Never try a style because someone else does it, always experiment until you find something that you know is your style for sure.
  • Always try on the item before buying it, because different stores measure their sizes differently.
  • Dress to please yourself and not society because you will feel so much more comfortable in it even if you're wearing stilettos.
